About Me

I am an Artificial Intelligence enthusiast, passionately learning about AI and machine learning since 2020. My journey has evolved with the ongoing innovations in the field.

Since 2022, I have been building projects focused on Large Language Models (LLMs), utilizing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and have experience fine-tuning existing models on custom datasets.

In addition to my work with LLMs, I have practical experience in computer vision, particularly in object detection and image generation. One of my key projects involved fine-tuning the YOLOv7 model on a medical dataset to accurately detect arteries and veins, for which I created boundary boxes using the LabelIMG tool.

My interest in AI expanded to model deployment, and I've deployed several models using AWS services like S3, EC2, Secrets Manager, and SageMaker. My hands-on experience with real-world datasets has been enriched through working with multiple startups, where I gained exposure to practical challenges and collaborative work environments. I'm eager to continue learning and contributing to the advancement of AI technologies.
